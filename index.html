Frequently Asks Questions

When you purchase a monthly or annual Webex Meeting plan, you get:

  1. One-click meetings: Launch meetings quickly from a variety of locations.
  2. HDFaces video conferencing: Share up to 25 high-definition video feeds per session.
  3. Built-in audio: Choose between VoIP and toll-based options at no extra charge.
  4. One-click recording: Send out crystal-clear recordings after your meetings.

Sign up for a free trial of Webex Meeting and enjoy access to all of our top-notch features. We will notify you before your trial expires, and you can decide to subscribe to a paid plan or let your trial expire.

Your free trial does not require a credit card. It will automatically cancel at the end of your trial period unless you have subscribed.

To cancel your paid subscription plan, sign in and go to the My Account page. Select Turn Off Renewal and Yes, Turn Off Renewal. Pre-paid service will continue until the end of your subscription period, at which time your plan will not renew.
